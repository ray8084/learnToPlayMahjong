Home Tiles
  • Bams are one of the three suits. Bam is short for Bamboo.
  • The tiles are mostly green and the images look like bamboo twigs.
  • Special Note: In this suit the 1 Bam tile is an image of a bird.
  • Bams are represented by a number 1 through 9 on the card. As shown, there are four of each number Bam tile.
