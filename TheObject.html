THE OBJECT

  • The object of the game is to form a winning hand of 14 tiles by making combinations of suits, numbers, Winds, Flowers and Dragons as shown on the NMJL card.
  • The NMJL Card probably looks like you are reading a foreign language. We will explain the Card and tiles used during this lesson.
  • You should all have a 2025 NMJL Card in hand which can be purchased from ModernMahjong.com or NationalMahJonggLeague.org. We strongly advise not to purchase on third party sites, there are many fake cards.
  • If you do not yet have your Card, you can still take part in Lesson One. We recommend you purchase before watching Lesson Two.
